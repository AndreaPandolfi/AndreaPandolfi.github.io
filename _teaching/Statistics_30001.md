Introduction to descriptive statistics and inference.

Description
======
[Statistics (30001)](https://didattica.unibocconi.eu/ts/tsn_anteprima.php?cod_ins=30001&anno=2024&ric_cdl=TR01&IdPag=7399) is a 2nd year course for the students enrolled in the CLEAM and CLEAF classes at Bocconi University. It introduces the basics of descriptive statistics and inference.


Intended Learning Outcomes (ILO)
======
- Properly summarize a dataset.
- Estimate and test hypotheses on the unknown parameters of a population on the basis of sample data.
- Build simple statistical models, such as regression models, aimed at studying the relationships  between variables of interest.
- Use the R software to address the aforementioned issues.
